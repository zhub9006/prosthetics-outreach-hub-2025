# Contributing to Prosthetics Outreach Hub 2025

## How to Help

### 1. Claim a Task
- Browse open Issues and comment to claim one
- Assign yourself to the task
- Add relevant labels (team, priority, category)

### 2. Task Categories
- **Outreach & Community Engagement** — Connect with local prosthetic clinics and advocacy groups
- **Clinical Trial Coordination** — Assist with NCT trial data collection and reporting
- **Fundraising & Grants** — Research funding opportunities and support grant applications
- **Logistics & Supply Chain** — Coordinate material and resource distribution
- **Communications & Marketing** — Manage social media, press releases, and outreach materials
- **Data Collection & Analysis** — Compile trial results, patient data, and outcome metrics

### 3. Commit Guidelines
- Use clear, descriptive commit messages
- Reference related issue numbers in your commits
- Keep PRs focused on a single task or feature

### 4. Security & Privacy
- Do not share patient-identifiable data in public repos
- Use encrypted channels for sensitive communications
- Follow HIPAA and IRB guidelines when handling clinical data
